Slide 4.1 · Lithium & Copper indices

Commodity prices are on an upward, volatile path

Lithium grows strongly from 2024 to 2025, while copper fluctuates around a higher plateau.

What the indices tell us
  • Lithium index rises from ~110 at the start of 2024 to more than 200 by end-2025.
  • Copper index stays closer to 105–120, but with frequent ups and downs.
  • This combination creates uncertainty on battery and electronics cost for Eurimobility.
