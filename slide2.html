Eurimobility – company and purchasing footprint

A European mid-sized manufacturer of electric-mobility components with a procurement spend that is central to value creation.

Eurimobility assembles Li-ion batteries, electronic control units and smart sensors for EV and micro-mobility OEMs. Procurement represents close to 60 % of revenue, making sourcing decisions a key strategic lever.

Procurement snapshot (2024 baseline)
Six main purchasing categories concentrated in electronics and batteries.
  • Electronic components – 105 M€ (34 % of total spend).
  • Li-ion batteries – 80 M€ (26 %).
  • Cables & connectors – 42 M€ (13 %).
  • Plastics & chassis – 35 M€ (11 %).
  • Logistics – 22 M€ (7 %).
  • Indirect purchases – 28 M€ (9 %).
Total purchases
312 M€
≈ 60 % of Eurimobility revenue.
