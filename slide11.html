Reducing Asian dependency on key technologies

Today, Asian sourcing still covers around half of electronic components and Li-ion batteries, with a lower but visible exposure on wiring & connectivity.

By 2026, the objective is to bring Asia’s share down to about 20 % overall, mainly by cutting dependency in Li-ion batteries, wiring/connectivity and electronic components through dual sourcing and new European partners.

32% → 20% Global Asia dependency between 2024 and 2026.
