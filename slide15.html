Jaggaer e-sourcing – Electronics, Cables, Batteries

Designing a multi-criteria reverse auction
to favour resilient EU suppliers

Eurimobility uses a weighted scoring model in Jaggaer: suppliers bid on price, but the final award also depends on quality, delivery, RSE and European localisation. The result: competitive prices and a more sovereign supply base.

Scoring model – weights used in the simulations
Balanced between cost, performance and ESG.
  • Price · 50%Final price after auction (vs initial RFQ).
  • Quality · 20%Supplier quality score (claims, ppm, audits).
  • RSE · 20%ESG / CSR score (CO₂, social, compliance).
  • Delivery · 10%Lead-time reliability & OTIF performance.
Key outcomes from the 3 auction simulations
Electronic components, wiring, Li-ion batteries.
  • In all three families, European suppliers rank #1 in the final weighted score.
  • Average savings of around 10–15 % vs initial price, while improving ESG and risk.
  • The multi-criteria model naturally favours competitive European partners instead of pure lowest-bid logic.
