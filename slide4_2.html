Slide 4.2 · Logistics & lead-time volatility

Logistics shocks amplify the total cost of Asian sourcing

Average Asia–Europe shipping time moved from stable to highly volatile.

What changed after the post-COVID crisis
  • Shipping costs increased up to ×10 at peak during the crisis.
  • Average Asia–EU lead times fluctuated between 42 and 63 days.
  • Instability drivers: Suez Canal incidents, Red Sea attacks, port congestion and FX volatility.
  • Asian sourcing now creates strong volatility on the total cost of ownership (TCO).
Impact
Unstable lead times & cost
Implication
Diversify away from Asia & maximise EU sourcing
