Scenario summary

Robustness of the 2026 roadmap

Benchmarking how leaders like Tesla and STMicro executed European relocalisation shows the playbook for Eurimobility: relocate strategic parts (modules, PCBs), tap public support, reduce Asian dependence, and build EU innovation partnerships.

Result: stable service, lower geopolitical risk, stronger resilience.
