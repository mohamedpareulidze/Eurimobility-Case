Reading the matrix
Four distinct sourcing strategies

Strategic categories (batteries & electronics) concentrate both value and risk. Leverage categories such as cables & connectors are strong candidates for competitive sourcing and reverse auctions, while simple and complex purchases call for efficiency and risk-control rather than pure cost focus.

Priority focus for Eurimobility
Where to act first
  • Build long-term, European partnerships for Li-ion batteries and electronic components.
  • Use reverse auctions on cables & connectors to unlock savings while diversifying suppliers.
  • Secure tooling, molds and capacity for plastics & chassis to avoid bottlenecks.
  • Streamline supplier base for logistics and indirect spend, focusing on service level and efficiency.
