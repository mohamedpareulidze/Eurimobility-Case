Slide 18 · Communication strategy
2026 – Internal & external messages

Communicating the sourcing transformation

The goal is to make the new European sourcing model clear, accepted and supported by employees, clients and suppliers, while managing expectations on cost and operations.

Key idea: simple, consistent messages are critical to secure buy-in and make the 2026 roadmap tangible for all stakeholders.
2026 communication is aligned with major sourcing milestones (auctions, awards, KPI reviews).
2026 internal plan
Employees · COMEX · Plants
  • Kick-off message from CEO / COMEX on the 2026 sourcing strategy.
  • Quarterly updates on progress vs. Asia 21% and >60% Europe.
  • Training waves on Jaggaer and new risk / ESG KPIs, with local feedback loops.
Internal milestones
Q1 2026
Kick-off, CEO message, first trainings.
Q2 2026
Auctions launched, dashboards shared.
Q3 2026
Review of results, fine-tuning of plans.
Q4 2026
Final KPI review and COMEX debrief.
2026 external plan
Clients · Suppliers · Institutions
  • Public announcement of the European sourcing strategy and ESG goals.
  • Customer communication on resilience, CO₂ footprint and compliance.
  • Targeted dialogue with key European and Asian suppliers on the transition.
External milestones
Q1 2026
Press release and customer briefings.
Q2 2026
Supplier webinars and onboarding.
Q3 2026
Case study with EU partners.
Q4 2026
ESG & sovereignty report published.
